About

Welcome to UR Spaces, this website was created by team U of Argo for CS476 in the Fall semester of 2023. This website is intended to help students with their needs at the University of Regina, in order to provide an easy and accessible way for them to easily ask questions, answer them for other students, and also to connect more with their community.
