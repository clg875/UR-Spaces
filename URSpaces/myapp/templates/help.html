Help

URSpaces utilizes subforums in order to allow users to find relevant posts that are related to what they are searching for, or similarly to post themselves and comment in related topics. Each subforum found in the main page will bring about numerous posts. To get an idea of what a post may be about, please look toward the title of the post. From there, if a post catches one's attention they can click onto it to get the full post and be able to comment on it by scrolling past other comments and inputting in the comment section.

To create one's own post, find the appropriate subforum from the home page, and click the create post button in the top right corner to bring down a menu for creating your post in the subforum. Make sure your post has a title and text for the post itself.

In order to like a post or comment, simply click on the like button on a post, to unlike, click the like button again to remove the like.

To change your profile, click on your username on the left sidebar in order to bring up your profile page. Here you can edit your bio, upload a photo for your profile image and view all posts you have made below.
